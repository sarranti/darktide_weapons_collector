Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the Darktide Weapons Collector application.

Data Collection and Usage

This application does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers. The app functions entirely within your web browser (client-side).

When you authorize the application to access your Google Sheets, the application only uses that access to read and update the weapon data within the specific spreadsheet you have chosen. All interactions with the Google Sheets API occur directly between your web browser and Google's servers.

No Backend Storage

We do not operate a backend database. We do not store your spreadsheet data, your personal identity information, or your Google account credentials. Any spreadsheet IDs you enter are stored locally in your browser's localStorage purely to maintain your settings for your next visit; this data never leaves your computer.

Third-Party Access

Your data is not shared with any third parties. We do not sell, rent, or provide access to your data to any advertisers or data brokers.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support email associated with the application's Google Cloud project.
